# Skill: Wiki Lint

## Goal

Maintain quality and consistency of the wiki.

## Checks

### 1. Structural Validity

* Has frontmatter?
* Required fields present?
* Valid types?

### 2. Consistency

* Conflicting statements across pages
* Duplicate concepts

### 3. Linking

* Orphan pages (no incoming links)
* Broken links

### 4. Freshness

* Old pages → mark `stale: true`

### 5. Confidence

* Low confidence pages flagged

## Actions

* Suggest merges
* Suggest updates
* Flag contradictions
* Mark stale content

## Output

* List of issues
* Suggested fixes
